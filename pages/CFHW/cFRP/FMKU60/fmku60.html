FMKU60 Module¶

Overview¶

The FMKU60 is an FPGA module that plugs into an *IBM cloudFPGA Research Platform*.

The card is equipped with a Kintex UltraScale XCKU060 from Xilinx and two banks of 8 GB of DDR4 memory.
